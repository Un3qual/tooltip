    Tooltips

    Tooltips is a simple tooltip tool (hehe) that skips the bullshit. Crafted with love using JQuery and CSS. It was designed with ease of use in mind.

    Just Get Started Already!

    It's super simple to start using Tooltips. Just add data-tooltip="tooltip content" to the element that you want to add the element to.

    Examples

    <p>I gobble down a row of Double Stuf <span data-tooltip="YUCK!">Golden</span> Oreos and a peanut butter sandwich, all sloshed down with a pint of chocolate milk. 

Later in the afternoon I stop at Five Guys to devour a <span data-tooltip="a fuck ton of bacon">double bacon</span> cheeseburger and a monster order of sodium-loaded fries.
